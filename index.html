Our Guyanese Heritage

The Knights family traces its roots to Guyana, on the northern coast of South America — the only English-speaking nation on the continent, known as the Land of Many Waters.

The national flag, the Golden Arrowhead, carries five meanings we carry with us: green for the forests and fields, gold for the nation's wealth and a bright future, red for zeal and sacrifice, black for endurance and perseverance, and white for the many rivers.
